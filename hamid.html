Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you work with?

I work with businesses of all sizes across various industries, including technology, finance, healthcare, e-commerce, and professional services. My expertise is particularly valuable for B2B companies, SaaS businesses, and organizations looking to establish thought leadership through content.

How do you measure the success of content?

I use a combination of quantitative and qualitative metrics including organic traffic, engagement rates (time on page, bounce rate), conversion rates, search rankings, social shares, and lead generation. For each project, we establish KPIs aligned with your business objectives before content creation begins.

What's your typical process for a new content project?

My process includes: 1) Discovery session to understand your goals and audience, 2) Competitive and gap analysis, 3) Content strategy development, 4) Keyword research and topic ideation, 5) Content creation, 6) Editing and optimization, 7) Performance tracking and iteration. The exact process may vary based on project scope.

How long does it take to see results from content marketing?

While some results (like social engagement) can be immediate, most content marketing efforts take 3-6 months to show significant traction in organic search results and lead generation. Consistent, high-quality content published over time yields compounding returns, which is why I recommend a minimum 6-month commitment for content strategy projects.

Do you offer ongoing content management services?

Yes, I offer monthly content management packages that include strategy, creation, publishing, and performance analysis. These ongoing relationships allow me to deeply understand your business and continuously optimize content performance based on data insights.
